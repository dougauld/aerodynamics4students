Wing Bending-Torsion Flutter Predictions
 
This form calculates the frequency solution for a 2 DoF bending/torsion model of a straight, high aspect ratio wing. The geometry of the wing along with its natural frequency and mass components is required so that airflow forced frequency may be computed. The solution may be purely real, indicating a stable oscillating solution or a complex root indicating an unstable solution.
Input Parameters
2 DoF Model Parameters
 
Wing Area(m2)

Wing Mass(Kg)

Wing Polar Inertia (Iα)

Distance to CG (xcg m)

Distance to AC (xac m)

Bending Natural Frequency (Hz)

Torsion Natural Frequency (Hz)

Maximum Airspeed (m/s)

Air Density (Kg/m3)
