NACA 4 or 5 Digit Milling Machine

This tool creates NACA 4 or 5 Digit section straight wings. The required inputs for the milling process are the NACA designation number, the number of data points to construct and the span of the wing. An optional section identification name can be entered so that this section can be retrieved at a later stage from amongst the many, many others kept in the store room. Note: all wings produced by this machine are 1m chord. A more modern section milling machine is available at NACA Calc 4,5,6 Sections.
